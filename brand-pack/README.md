## Mitra at a glance

- **Name:** Mitra (MEE-tra). Persian / Sanskrit for "friend, ally."
- **Pronoun:** **she.** Not "it." Hard rule.
- **What we are:** an AI tutoring companion for middle and high school students with ADHD and learning differences, delivered in the messaging apps they already use (WhatsApp, Telegram, KakaoTalk).
- **What we're not:** a homework-completion service, a parent surveillance tool, or "just another AI chatbot."

## Primary brand color

Teal `#2A9D8F` on light backgrounds. Dark teal `#21867A` for navigation and footer.

## Primary font

DM Sans (Google Fonts). All weights from 400 to 800.
