# Mitra color palette

The full palette, with hex values, intended use, and inconsistency notes.

## Primary teal (the brand color)

The teal family is the visual anchor. The CTA color is `#2A9D8F`. Everything else in this family is a shade of it.

| Swatch | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Teal | `#2A9D8F` | Every primary CTA. The "Mitra" wordmark color on the home page. Accent emphasis ("loves" highlights). |
| Dark teal | `#21867A` | Site-wide top navigation background. Site-wide footer background. CTA hover state. |
| Hover teal | `#1F7A6E` | Hover state on some surfaces where dark teal would be too heavy. |
| Border teal | `#1A6D63` | Bottom border on the dark-teal nav. |
| Soft teal | `#6BBE9F` | Soft section backgrounds (closing CTA gradient half). |

## Navy (secondary)

Navy is the contrast color. Used for headings on lighter pages and for surfaces that need authority without competing with the teal CTA.

| Swatch | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Primary navy | `#2B4C7E` | Headings, links on lighter pages. |
| Dark navy | `#1B3A5C` | Promise lines on pain landing pages. Contrast text on yellow backgrounds. Secondary CTA fill. |
| Navy tint | `#D6E1EE` | Badges, highlights. |

## Kakao yellow (accent)

The yellow ties to KakaoTalk chat-bubble convention. Used on student-side chat bubbles in product mockups, as an accent pill color, and as the "notepaper" feel on the pain landing variant for "the kid who shrugs."

| Swatch | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Kakao yellow | `#FEE500` | Chat-bubble fill (student-side). Accent pills. "Mitra Solution" highlights. |
| Yellow border | `#C9B600` | Outline on yellow pills to give them shape. |
| Yellow cream | `#FFF7E6` | Warm notepaper backgrounds. Label pill fills. |

## Coral (emphasis)

Coral is the warm energetic accent. Used sparingly for emphasis, strike-throughs, and personality.

| Swatch | Hex | Use |
|---|---|---|
| Coral | `#E8736B` | Marketing emphasis, accent details. |

> **Inconsistency to fix:** an older `#E76F51` coral appears in one strike-through. Standardize on `#E8736B` going forward.

## Color usage rules

1. **CTAs are always teal (`#2A9D8F`).** Hover state goes to dark teal (`#21867A`). The navy CTA used on the pain landing pb-5 variant is an exception explained in the brand guidelines; do not introduce navy CTAs elsewhere.
2. **The dark teal `#21867A` is reserved for navigation and footer.** Don't use it on body content backgrounds.
3. **Kakao yellow is for accent only.** Never a primary background or a CTA. Headings on yellow use dark navy (`#1B3A5C`) for contrast, not teal.
4. **Coral is sparing.** One coral accent per page max. Coral is energy, not structure.
5. **Backgrounds are calm.** When in doubt, mint, lavender, or white. The pain landing notepaper yellow is reserved for the "kid who shrugs" treatment and equivalent moments.
