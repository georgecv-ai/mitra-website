For Teaching Partners

Bringing Mitra to North Georgia

We're building a small group of partners, homeschool communities, independent tutors, and learning support practitioners, to bring Mitra to families in North Georgia. If you work with families in towns like Toccoa, Clarkesville, Cornelia, Hartwell, or anywhere across the region, and you've watched kids fall through the gaps of what's available to them, we'd like to talk.

Who Mitra is

Mitra is a patient AI tutoring companion who works with kids over text message, on the student's phone via the text app Telegram. She was built first for kids with ADHD, because if something works for a kid whose attention doesn't arrive on schedule, it works for any kid. Short sessions. No scheduling. A tutor who remembers what your student cares about and picks up where they left off.

What a partnership looks like

You stay in charge of your students. Mitra works with your students between your sessions. Your curriculum, your methodology, your relationship. Mitra doesn't replace you, she extends your reach while giving the student another path to support.

Mitra can work off of your materials. Mitra goes where the student goes, she works on what your student wants to work on. When that's the math sheet you sent home, the chapter you assigned, or the problem set you handed out, Mitra has it. Partners upload their materials into Mitra's bookshelf, so when a student says "I'm stuck on the worksheet," it's your worksheet she's helping them with. If you don't have set materials, Mitra can also draw on Common Core, Advanced Placement, and International Baccalaureate standards.

You see signal, not surveillance. You see how your materials are landing across your students, what's clicking, what's creating friction, what students are asking about. You don't see individual session transcripts. Children deserve privacy with their learning companion, the same way they would with a thoughtful human tutor.

What you get

More students without more hours. If you're at capacity, Mitra lets you hold more families. She handles the daily practice between your sessions, so the hours you spend one-on-one become the high-value hours not the repetition.

$5 per student, every month. For every family you refer, you earn $5 per month per student, every month they're with us. A partner with fifteen families earns $900 a year, passively, on top of her regular work. No caps. No tiers. Alternatively, you can pass that on as a $5 monthly discount, and we will honor that for life. Your choice.

Mitra Tutoring Advisor, free. Every partner gets access to a version of Mitra built for you, not your students. She'll help you plan lesson ladders, think through how to approach a specific student, and draw on what's working across our partner network. Think of her as a consulting colleague who doesn't charge hourly.

A real voice in what we build next. You'll shape what Mitra becomes. Your feedback, your materials, your reporting needs, they go directly to the people building her.

What we're offering early partners

We're being honest: this is new. We're starting in North Georgia because I know these communities, and we want two things from our first partners: families we can serve well, and feedback we can build on. Early partners get:

  • Personal onboarding, including help setting up your bookshelf and walking through your first few referrals
  • Direct access to the company CEO when something isn't working the way you expected
  • The discount or commission terms above, locked in permanently
  • First look at new features, and real input on what we build for communities like yours

This is a small group by design. We'd rather work closely with a few partners we can support well than broadcast a generic offer to hundreds.

Who this is for

  • Homeschool co-op coordinators
  • Independent tutors and learning specialists
  • Reading specialists and speech/language practitioners
  • Educational ministries and church-based learning programs
  • Small academies with flexible procurement

If you're not on this list but you serve families in North Georgia and this resonates, reach out anyway. We'd rather have a conversation and figure out if there's a fit than make you guess.

A note from the founder

I'm George. I grew up in Toccoa.

Building this product is personal as is trying to help kids and families back home.
