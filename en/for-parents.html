Every session starts the same way: "Hey, how's it going?" The tutor asks about their day, what they're into, what's bugging them. This isn't small talk — it's the whole method.

For kids with ADHD, the relationship with the tutor IS the intervention. If a session looks like "just chatting" — that means it's working. Kids don't learn from people they don't trust.

Your kid will learn more from a tutor they actually want to talk to.

It's not just about homework

If your kid comes in talking about a bad day, a fight with a friend, or just feeling off — the tutor rolls with it. No "sorry, I can only help with schoolwork."

For kids with ADHD, there's no clean line between "school stuff" and "life stuff." Forcing that boundary just kills trust. The tutor listens, validates, and steers back to learning when the moment is right.

The only hard line is safety — if the tutor picks up on anything concerning, you'll hear from us immediately.

You'll tell us which subjects your kid needs help with. We use those as starting points — but if they get fired up about something else, the tutor goes with it. A productive session on any topic is a win. Your weekly summary tells you exactly what they worked on.

  • Sessions happen on Telegram — your kid texts the tutor like they'd text a friend. WhatsApp is available for existing testers only.
  • Messages are short — 2-4 sentences max. No walls of text. ADHD brains don't need more.
  • One idea at a time. No information dumps.
  • The tutor reads your kid's energy. Locked in? Let's go. Goofing around? Stay light, find the opening.
  • Most sessions run 10-15 minutes. Short and focused beats long and lost.
  • Every complete session ends on a win — even on a rough day.

What we measure (and what we don't)

No grades. No test scores. We track growth signals: Is your kid more willing and able to try harder problems? Are they staying in sessions longer? Coming back on their own? That's what matters.

Mitra can help your child with almost any topic they'll hit in school — if it's on a syllabus somewhere, Mitra can work with it.

That said, Mitra is extensively trained in five core subjects for grades 6 through 12:

  • Math — from pre-algebra through calculus
  • Reading — comprehension, analysis, close reading
  • Writing — structure, argument, editing
  • Science — biology, chemistry, physics, earth science
  • Social Studies — history, geography, civics

In these subjects, Mitra doesn't just know the content — she's been trained on the specific ways students get stuck at each grade level, and she knows how to approach an explanation from a different direction when the first one doesn't land.

When you enroll, you tell us which subjects your child is working on and their current level. Mitra focuses there first, and your child can always ask about other things too.

You see progress summaries, not conversation content. That's on purpose.

Your kid needs a space where they can say "I don't get this" or "today sucked" without worrying you're reading over their shoulder. That safety is what makes the tutoring work. If there's ever a real safety concern, you hear from us immediately.

When we contact you

  • Milestones: When your kid hits a learning goal or makes real progress.
  • Weekly summaries: What your kid worked on, what clicked, and how they're engaging — including when they picked a different subject than what you signed up for.
  • Safety concerns: If the tutor picks up on anything suggesting self-harm, abuse, or danger — you get an immediate notification. Automated and instant.
