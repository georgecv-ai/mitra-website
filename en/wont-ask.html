MITRA frequently asked questions

Is there really a free trial?

Yes. The first 7 days are free. If Mitra isn't the right fit for your child, cancel within 7 days and you won't be charged. There is no minimum commitment.

How is Mitra different from ChatGPT?

Mitra is designed as a relationship-based learning tutor, not a general answer engine. She remembers your child, adapts to how they speak and what they care about, and walks through the process together instead of just handing out answers.

What subjects does she cover?

Whatever your child is working on. Mitra teaches against the U.S. Common Core, the Korean national curriculum, and the International Baccalaureate (IB) from 6th grade through 12th grade. That includes math, science, history, writing, foreign languages, anything coming home from school.
