You're enrolled. Now let's get your child talking to Mitra.

It takes about two minutes. Your child opens the app you already use, sends one short code to link the account, and they're in. Here's exactly what to do.

Your child's enrollment code

MITRA-XXXX

This code links the chat to your child's account. It's already built into the buttons and QR codes below for WhatsApp — for KakaoTalk and Telegram, your child sends it once when they first message Mitra.

First, grab your enrollment code

When you signed up, we showed you a short enrollment code — it looks like MITRA-XXXX. Your child sends this to Mitra the first time they message her, and it links the chat to their account.

Can't find it? Sign in to the parent portal to see it again, or email support@mitratutor.com and we'll resend it.

Pick your messaging app

Use whichever one your child already texts on. The steps are slightly different for each.

KakaoTalk Most popular in Korea
  1. On your child's phone, open the button or scan the QR code to add the Mitra channel (채널 추가).
  2. Tap into the chat and have your child send a message first — anything, even just "hi."
  3. When Mitra asks, send your enrollment code (your code above). That links the account, and you're ready to go.
Important: On KakaoTalk, Mitra can't message your child first — that's a Kakao rule. Your child always has to send the first message. After that, conversation flows normally.

Telegram Press Start to turn Mitra on
  1. On your child's phone, open the button or scan the QR code to open the @Mitradhdbot chat.
  2. Tap the big START button. This is the step that turns Mitra on — until your child presses Start, Telegram won't let her send a single message.
  3. If we matched your child's Telegram username at signup, Mitra greets them right away. If not, just send your enrollment code (your code above) and she'll link the account.
This is the #1 thing people miss: Telegram is the only app where Mitra can check in first — but only after your child has pressed Start that one time. No Start, no messages.

Not sure which to use?

  • In Korea? KakaoTalk is what most families use — your child already lives in it.
  • Outside Korea? WhatsApp or Telegram are the easy picks.
  • Want Mitra to gently check in? Telegram is the only app where she can start the conversation — after the first Start.

Pick one to start. You can always switch later — just message Mitra on the new app with the same code.

If something's not working

Mitra isn't replying (or never messaged us)

On Telegram: your child has to press the START button once. Telegram blocks every message from Mitra until they do — so if she's gone silent, that Start almost certainly hasn't happened yet.

On KakaoTalk: your child has to send the first message — Mitra can't start the chat. Make sure they've added the channel and sent something.

On any app: double-check the enrollment code was sent and typed correctly. Still stuck? Email support@mitratutor.com and we'll link the account manually.

I lost my enrollment code

Sign in to the parent portal to view it again, or email support@mitratutor.com and we'll resend it.

Mitra says she doesn't recognize my child

This usually means the chat isn't linked to the account yet. Have your child send the enrollment code again. On WhatsApp, make sure they're messaging from the phone number you gave us at signup.

Still stuck? We'll sort it out — email support@mitratutor.com.

Can my child use more than one app?

Stick to one app for the best experience — that way Mitra keeps everything in one continuous conversation. If you do need to switch, just message her on the new app with the same enrollment code.

What happens next

The first session is just a conversation — Mitra gets to know your child before any teaching starts. That's by design. The first few sessions build the relationship; the learning grows from there.
