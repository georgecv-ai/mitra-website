What makes this different

Most tutoring assumes your kid can sit still for an hour on someone else's schedule. That doesn't work for a lot of kids — especially kids with ADHD. Mitra is built around how they already use their phone: short texts, on their own time, with a tutor that knows their name and picks up where they left off.
