How Mitra works

Not a list of features. A short set of principles, with what they actually look like in the work.

I built Mitra because my daughter and I both have ADHD, and we had to figure out what works for us. We also learned that when something works for a kid whose attention doesn't arrive on schedule, it works for any kid.

Mitra means companion. That's not marketing. It's the whole idea. Learning is easier with a companion.
