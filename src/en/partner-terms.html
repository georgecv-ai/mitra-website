For Teaching Partners

How our partner program works

If you're considering becoming a Mitra Partner, through our North Georgia program or directly, this page explains the practical terms in plain language. The full legal agreement and guidelines are linked at the bottom; you'll sign these when you onboard.

The two ways to be paid

You choose one of two reward modes when you sign up:

Commission. $5 USD per student per month, paid quarterly, for every family you refer who's actively subscribed. Founding Partners (signed on or before December 15, 2026) keep this rate locked in for both existing and future referrals.

Discount. Instead of taking the $5 yourself, you can pass it on to your families as a $5/month lifetime discount on their subscription. Their subscription is cheaper, you don't deal with W-9s or 1099s, and you still get all the other Mitra benefits.

You pick one mode for your account. Families already enrolled keep the terms they enrolled under.

What's required before we can pay commission

If you choose commission mode, before we can pay you anything we need:

  1. A signed Partner Agreement, accepted via the signup form (typed name + checkbox + timestamp).
  2. A completed W-9 (for US-based partners). Download the W-9 form here, fill it out, and email it to Admin@mitratutor.com.

Once both are received, your status flips to "active" and you can start sharing your partner code.

If you choose discount mode, you only need the signed Agreement. No W-9, no 1099, no annual tax forms.

Privacy: what you see, and what you don't

You see how your uploaded materials are landing across the students you've referred, what's clicking, what's creating friction. You don't see individual session transcripts or identifying information about specific kids. Children deserve privacy with their tutor, the same way they would with a thoughtful human one.

Materials you upload

You keep ownership of what you upload. While our Agreement is in effect, we have a license to use your materials with your referred students. When the Agreement ends, materials are removed from the bookshelf within 90 days, or 30 days if you ask us specifically (email Admin@mitratutor.com).

A note on payments and US tax

Commissions to US-based partners are paid by The Coaching Record LLC, Mitra's US service delivery partner. Payments are processed through Stripe. If you earn $600 or more in a calendar year, you'll receive a 1099-NEC in January from The Coaching Record (not from Mitra AI directly). Your contractual relationship is with Mitra AI; service delivery and payments in the US run through The Coaching Record.
