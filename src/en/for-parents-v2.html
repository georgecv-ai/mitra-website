Connection first. Curriculum second.

Every session starts the same way: "Hey, how's it going?" That isn't small talk. That's the whole method.

01

For kids with ADHD, the relationship is the intervention. If a session looks like "just chatting," that means it's working. Kids don't learn from people they don't trust.

02

Sessions happen on KakaoTalk, Telegram, or WhatsApp — your kid texts the tutor like they'd text a friend. No new app to install.

03

Messages are short. Two to four sentences max. ADHD brains don't need walls of text.

04

The tutor reads your kid's energy. Locked in? Let's go. Goofing around? Stay light, find the opening.

05

Most sessions run 10 to 15 minutes. Short and focused beats long and lost.

RESULT

Every complete session ends on a win, even on a rough day. Growth signals (engagement, willingness to try harder problems, coming back on their own) are what we track — not grades.
