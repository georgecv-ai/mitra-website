For Hagwons, Schools, and Tutors

Mitra works with hagwons, schools, and solo tutors who want a tutoring AI that helps their students between sessions, on the platforms they already use (KakaoTalk, Telegram).

What we offer partners

  • An admin portal to upload your textbooks, worksheets, and class materials
  • Class-level and org-level file scopes — Mitra references your materials with the right students
  • Aggregate curriculum analytics: what's being used, where students commonly struggle
  • A peer-mode chat with Mitra to brainstorm pedagogy, lesson sequences, and learner profiles

Our principles (the partnership filter)

We have a few non-negotiables. If you can't agree to these, we're not the right fit, and we will say so directly.

1. We require parent email and phone for every student.

No exceptions. Reports, harm escalation, and account transfer all depend on it. If a student is enrolled through your hagwon, we still need a real parent contact.

2. We own the Mitra-student relationship.

If a student leaves your hagwon, they continue with Mitra. You don't get to terminate the student's account. Their parent owns that decision.

3. We report curriculum analytics, never individual student data, to partners.

You see aggregate patterns across your students (what's being used, where the group struggles). You do not see per-student session details, bridge outcomes, memory notes, or engagement metrics. Per-student reports go to parents only. If a parent chooses to share with you, that's between them and you.

4. Mitra never reports directly to a partner about a specific student.

Even if you ask. Even if it would be convenient. Even if you have a contract clause. The student-tutor relationship has to be safe.

5. We will walk from a partner whose incentives don't align with the student's interest.

Including ours. If a partnership starts pulling Mitra in a direction that hurts the kid we're supposed to be helping, we end it.
